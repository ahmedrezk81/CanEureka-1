Who We Are

A Trusted Bridge Between Canada and the World

CanEureka Global is a Canadian consulting and strategic advisory firm based in Ontario, specializing in international educational collaboration, institutional partnerships, and professional advisory services.

Our team combines deep expertise in partnership development, international engagement, institutional relations, operational coordination, and strategic business development.
